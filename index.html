About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must fight against endless waves of monsters. Test your skills and strategy as you progress through increasingly challenging levels.

Key Features:

  • Endless waves of challenging monsters
  • Multiple character abilities and upgrades
  • Dynamic combat system
  • Progressive difficulty scaling
  • Engaging survival mechanics

How to Play:

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Survive as long as possible while collecting power-ups and defeating monsters. Upgrade your abilities and adapt your strategy as the game becomes more challenging.
